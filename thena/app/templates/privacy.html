Privacy

We take privacy very seriously.

Thena is not interested in tracking you in order to monetize you. We store the least possible information in order to make this project work -- currently, just your email.

The only human who has access to any of your data is Daniel, the creator of Thena. You can get in touch with him at daniel {at} thena {dot} io. He's really not interested in your specific history; he just wants enough data to build some cool machine learning models.

There is a "snooze" feature currently in development which will let you disable Thena for a few minutes if you'd rather she not see a few of your searches. It's top priority over at HQ.
